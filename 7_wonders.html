7 Wonders

A card-drafting civilization-building game for 3-7 players. The game unfolds over three "Ages," each with its own deck of cards. The objective is to accumulate the most victory points by the end of the third Age.

Gameplay Overview (Per Age)

Each Age consists of 6 turns.

  1. Deal Cards: At the beginning of each Age, each player is dealt 7 cards from the corresponding Age deck.
  2. Choose a Card: Players simultaneously choose one card from their hand to keep.
  3. Perform Action: Simultaneously, all players reveal their chosen cards and perform one of three actions:
    • Build a Structure: Pay the card's cost and place it in your city.
    • Build a Wonder Stage: Pay the Wonder stage's cost and discard a card to build it.
    • Discard for 3 Coins: Discard a card to gain 3 coins.
  4. Pass Cards: Players pass their remaining hand of cards to a neighbor (direction changes each Age).
  5. Repeat: Continue until all cards have been played.
  6. End of Age Military Conflict: Compare military strength with your neighbors.

End of Game and Scoring

The game ends after the third Age. Victory points are tallied from seven categories: Wonder, Treasury, Military, Blue Cards (Civilian), Yellow Cards (Commerce), Green Cards (Science), and Purple Cards (Guilds).

The player with the most victory points wins.
