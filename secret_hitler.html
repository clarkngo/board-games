Secret Hitler

A social deduction game for 5-10 players where participants are secretly divided into two teams: Liberals and Fascists, with one player being Secret Hitler.

Roles and Objectives

  • Liberals: Enact 5 Liberal Policies OR assassinate Hitler.
  • Fascists: Enact 6 Fascist Policies OR elect Hitler as Chancellor after 3 Fascist Policies have been enacted.
  • Hitler: Plays for the Fascist team.

Gameplay (Rounds)

Each round consists of three phases: Election, Legislative Session, and Executive Action.

  1. Election: The President nominates a Chancellor, and all players vote.
  2. Legislative Session: The President draws 3 policies, discards one, and passes two to the Chancellor. The Chancellor enacts one policy.
  3. Executive Action: If a Fascist Policy is enacted, the President gains a special power.

Game End

  • Liberal Victory: 5 Liberal Policies enacted OR Hitler is assassinated.
  • Fascist Victory: 6 Fascist Policies enacted OR Hitler is elected Chancellor after 3 Fascist Policies have been enacted.
