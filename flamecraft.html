Flamecraft

A charming and strategic board game where players take on the role of Flamekeepers, working with magical dragons to craft enchanted items and enhance shops. The objective is to earn the most reputation points by enchanting shops, gathering resources, and strategically placing dragons.

Game Setup

  1. Village Board: Place the Village Board (Town Mat) in the center of the playing area.
  2. Shop Cards: Shuffle the Shop Cards and place six of them face up on the designated spaces on the board. Some starter shops have lighter colored backs and are placed first.
  3. Dragon Deck (Artisan Dragons): Shuffle the Artisan Dragon cards to form a draw pile and place it next to the Fountain. Deal three Artisan Dragon cards to each player as their starting hand.
  4. Enchantment Deck: Shuffle the Enchantment Cards (purple deck recommended for beginners) and place them near the board. Deal out five face-up enchantment cards.
  5. Resource Tokens (Goods): Place the resource tokens (bread, potions, iron, etc.) in piles where all players can access them. Coins are placed on the fountain area.
  6. Reputation Track: Each player chooses a player token (dragon) and takes the matching-color Reputation marker. Place all Reputation markers on the starting space of the reputation track.
  7. Fancy Dragons: Deal each player two Fancy Dragon cards. Each player chooses one to keep and returns the other to the bottom of the deck.
  8. First Player: Determine the first player using your preferred method. If playing with 4 or more players, the 4th and 5th players start with one goods token of their choice.

Gameplay

The game is played over a series of turns, with each player taking one turn at a time, moving clockwise. Each turn consists of the following phases:

  1. Visit a Shop: Move your dragon token to a different shop than the one you visited last turn. If another player is already at the chosen shop, you must pay them one goods token or a coin. If you cannot pay, you must choose an unoccupied shop.
  2. Choose an Action: After visiting a shop, you must choose to either Gather resources or Enchant the shop.

Gathering Resources

  • Gain all the goods, coins, or other bonuses shown on the shop card, any enchantment cards tucked under the shop, and any Artisan Dragon cards at that shop.
  • Optionally, place an Artisan Dragon from your hand onto an empty slot at the shop.
  • Optionally, "fire up" one of the dragons at the shop to use its magical ability.
  • Optionally, perform the shop's ability if it has one.

Enchanting a Shop

  • Play one of your Enchantment Cards that matches the shop's icon and pay the required resources (coins can be used as wild resources).
  • Gain the rewards listed on the enchantment card and tuck it under the top of the shop. This adds another good of that type for future gathering.
  • Optionally, "fire up" all the dragons at that shop to use their abilities.

End of the Game

The game ends when either the Artisan Dragon deck or the Enchantment deck runs out. All players, including the one who triggered the end, get one final turn.

Scoring

Players add to their score:

  • Reputation points gained throughout the game.
  • One reputation point for every coin they have left in their supply.
  • Points from their secret Fancy Dragon goals.

The player with the most reputation points wins!
