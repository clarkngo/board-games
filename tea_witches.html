Tea Witches

A worker placement game where 2-4 players, acting as tea vendors, compete over four rounds (days) to prove their kettle has the mettle to win the annual Teaquinox Faire. The goal is to gain loyalty from witches, upgrade your tea hut, and collect prestige by serving customers and managing resources.

Game Objective

Become the greatest tea witch by accumulating the most prestige points over four rounds. You earn prestige by serving witches, upgrading your hut, attracting Celebri-Teas, and completing to-go orders.

Game Flow (Four Rounds/Days)

Each day consists of two main phases:

  1. Summoning Phase:
    • Players simultaneously draw witch cards from their personal decks to form a queue outside their tea hut.
    • This is a "push your luck" element: you can keep drawing cards for more potential rewards, but if you draw two witches of the same "coven" (suit), you get "hexed," causing witches to leave your queue.
  2. Service Phase:
    • Players take turns sending their magical "TeaPups" to various "Bootiques" in the city.
    • At Bootiques, TeaPups can gather toppings, collect tea from the rotating crystal fountain, acquire powerful upgrades for your hut, attract "Celebri-Teas," or brew "Special-Teas" with bonus abilities.
    • The collected resources are used to serve the witches waiting in your queue, fulfilling their tea orders and gaining rewards.

Winning the Game

After four rounds, the game ends, and players score prestige for loyal customers, upgrades, filled to-go orders, and leftover resources. The player with the most prestige wins.
