Splendor

Splendor is a popular board game where players take on the role of Renaissance merchants, collecting gems to acquire development cards and attract nobles to earn prestige points. The goal is to be the first player to reach 15 prestige points.

Setup

  1. Gem Tokens: Separate the gem tokens by color and place them in piles within reach of all players.
  2. Development Cards: Shuffle each of the three levels of development cards separately. Place them in three face-down decks.
  3. Face-up Cards: Reveal four cards from each deck and place them face-up in a row next to their respective decks.
  4. Noble Tiles: Shuffle the noble tiles and place a number of them face-up equal to the number of players plus one.

Gameplay

On their turn, a player must choose and perform only one of the following four actions:

  1. Take Gem Tokens:
    • Take three different colored gem tokens.
    • OR, take two gem tokens of the same color (if there are at least four tokens of that color).
  2. Reserve One Development Card and Take One Gold Token:
    • Take a development card into your hand (you can have up to three reserved cards).
    • Take one gold token (wild card).
  3. Purchase One Development Card:
    • Pay the cost of a face-up development card or one you have reserved.
    • Development cards provide permanent gem bonuses for future purchases.

Nobles

At the end of your turn, if you have enough gem bonuses from your purchased development cards to attract a noble, you take that noble's tile for prestige points.

End of the Game

The game ends when one player reaches at least 15 prestige points. The current round is completed so that every player has had an equal number of turns. The player with the most prestige points wins.
