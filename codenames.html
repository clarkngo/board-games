Codenames

A word-association party game where two teams compete to identify all of their team's secret agents before the other team does.

Setup

  1. Divide into Teams: Split players into two teams (Red and Blue).
  2. Choose Spymasters: Each team selects one player to be their Spymaster.
  3. Lay Out Codenames: Place 25 word cards in a 5x5 grid.
  4. Choose a Key Card: Spymasters randomly choose a key card, which shows the secret identities of the words.

Gameplay

  1. Spymaster Gives a Clue: The Spymaster gives a one-word clue and a number (e.g., "Animals 3").
  2. Field Operatives Guess: The team guesses words they think relate to the clue.
  3. If they guess correctly, they can guess again.
  4. If they guess a word for the other team, their turn ends.
  5. If they guess the assassin, their team loses immediately.

Winning the Game

The first team to identify all of their agents wins.
