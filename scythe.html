Scythe

Scythe is an engine-building board game set in an alternate-history 1920s Europa, where players lead factions to gain wealth and power. The ultimate goal is to have the most coins at the end of the game.

Objective

The game ends when any player places their sixth star on the Triumph Track. The player with the most coins at this point wins.

Turn Structure

Players take turns clockwise. On your turn, place your action token on a different section of your Player Mat than where it was on your previous turn. You can perform the top-row action, the bottom-row action, both (top then bottom), or neither.

Actions Overview

  • Move: Move your character and/or mechs.
  • Produce: Gain resources or workers from territories you control.
  • Trade: Spend 1 coin to gain two resources or increase your popularity.
  • Bolster: Gain power or combat cards.
  • Upgrade: Improve your actions and reduce costs.
  • Deploy: Place a mech to gain a new ability.
  • Build: Place a structure for a bonus.
  • Enlist: Gain a one-time bonus and a recurring bonus.

Combat

Combat occurs when a character or mech moves into a territory occupied by an opponent's character or mech. Both players secretly choose power on their Power Dial and can add Combat cards. The higher value wins.

End of Game and Scoring

The game ends immediately when a player places their sixth star. Final scores are calculated based on coins, stars, territories, resources, and structure bonuses, all multiplied by your popularity level.
