Bang! The Walking Dead

A card game that re-themes the classic "Bang!" game with characters and factions from "The Walking Dead" universe.

Objective

Each player has a secret role with a specific objective:

  • Leader: Eliminate all Saviors and the Loner.
  • Saviors: Secretly try to kill the Leader.
  • Survivors: Secretly help and protect the Leader.
  • Loner: Secretly aims to be the last player alive.

Player Turn

Each player's turn consists of three phases:

  1. Draw Two Cards: Draw the top two cards from the draw pile.
  2. Play Any Number of Cards: Play cards from your hand to help yourself or attack other players.
  3. Discard Excess Cards: At the end of your turn, discard cards until the number of cards in your hand matches your current health points.

Combat and Elimination

  • When a player loses their last health point, they are eliminated from the game.
  • If the Leader accidentally eliminates a Survivor, the Leader must discard all cards in hand and in play.
  • Any player eliminating a Savior draws three cards as a reward.

The game continues until one of the victory conditions for a role is met.
